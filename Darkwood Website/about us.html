About Us Page

Continue below to learn about the Darkwood game!

Atmosphere

Darkwood is a gripping horror game that plunges you into a foreboding, otherworldly forest. With a unique top-down perspective, atmospheric visuals, and immersive sound design, Darkwood creates an unnerving and suspenseful atmosphere that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

The Setting

Set in an eerie, post-apocalyptic Poland, it challenges you to survive the nightmarish horrors that lurk within. Its non-linear gameplay and branching narratives offer multiple paths and choices, shaping the outcome of your character's journey.

What You Do

As an unnamed protagonist, you must navigate through a dark and twisted landscape, scavenging for resources, crafting tools, and fortifying your hideout against the encroaching darkness. Prepare for a haunting adventure that will test your bravery and sanity.

Who Made It

Darkwood was developed by a small group of indie game developers under the name 'Acid Wizard Studios' Each of them were tasked with creating certain aspects of the game respectfully, dividing the workload relatively equally among themselves.

Enemies

Darkwood features a large variety of unnerving enemies. The Banshee, a ghostly figure emitting a deadly scream. The Chomper, a grotesque and fierce creature, ready to ambush. And many, many more await.
